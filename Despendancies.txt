Spotify Data Analysis

The dependencies of the project is
Pandas
Spotipy
MySQL Connector
SpotifyClientCredentials from spotipy.oauth2

From the view of a database,
MySQL Workbench (preferred) or Command line client

From the view of spotify,
A account on spotify
An App from Spotify Developer for client_id and client_secret key

From the view of data,
The primary data is the URIs of each songs that you want to analyse, which are gathered here in the ani_track.txt file, You can create your own set of song lists from the spotify website, by capturing and storing the URI.
